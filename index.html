Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about our engineering and maintenance services.

How much does your service cost?
The cost depends on the type of service required. Simple repairs are usually affordable, while more complex installations may vary in price. We always provide a free quote before starting any job.
Do you handle emergency services?
Yes, we offer emergency services. If you have an urgent issue, contact us immediately and we’ll respond as quickly as possible.
How long does a typical job take?
Most standard jobs are completed within a few hours. Larger or more technical projects may take longer, but we always provide a clear time estimate before starting.
Do you provide materials for the job?
Yes, we can supply high-quality materials if needed. All costs are clearly explained in your quote so there are no surprises.
Do your services come with a warranty?
Yes, we stand behind our work. We offer a service guarantee to ensure your issue is properly resolved.
Which areas do you cover?
We operate across Lagos and nearby areas including Lekki, Ikeja, Yaba, Surulere, and more. Contact us to confirm availability in your location.
Can I request a service via WhatsApp?
Absolutely. You can reach us via WhatsApp, call, or this website. You can even send photos or videos for a quick assessment.
